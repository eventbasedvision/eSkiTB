Teaser

Event streams naturally suppress static overlays and clutter in broadcast ski footage.

Dataset

Event-based reformulation of SkiTB with dense annotations and attributes.

Content

  • Events: HDF5 `events` with `(t, x, y, p)` in microseconds.
  • Annotations: `[x, y, w, h]` pixels, class `Skier`; 1 ms spline labels.
  • Attributes: occlusion, fast motion, illumination variation, background clutter, and more.
  • Splits: Train 240 / Val 30 / Test 30 sequences.

Stats

Heavy-tailed event rates, skewed scales, and dense motion during take-off and flight.

Why events

Screen-space overlays vanish in the event domain; physical clutter follows background flow.

RGB trackers drift on broadcast overlays and banners.

v2e preserves skier silhouette and motion; used for eSkiTB conversion.

Iso-informational pipeline

Raw broadcast frames -> v2e -> events; no SuperSloMo or neural upsampling.

Evaluation

Transformer parity: STARK (RGB) vs SDTrack (event) on identical splits.

Headline numbers

  • SDTrack (finetuned): 0.711 IoU, +0.399 over its pretrained baseline.
  • STARK (fine-tuned RGB): 0.795 IoU; ski-specific: 0.829 IoU.
  • High-clutter split: SDTrack 0.685 IoU (+20.0 over generic STARK).

Voxelized to 128x128 for current SNN limits; dataset supports full 1280x720.

Validation peaks around epoch 20; used as release checkpoint.
